Amazon offers you a variety of ways to return your item fulfilled by Amazon:

DHL Express drop off:
Drop-off services at convenient locations (available in select countries). Find a
DHL drop-off location at
https://locator.dhl.com
DHL Express pickup:
Pickup services at the location, time, and place most convenient for you (available
in select countries).
