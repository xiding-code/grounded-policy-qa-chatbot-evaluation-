Return Shipping Cost
You can return eligible item(s) through
Your Orders.
When you choose the shipping method for your return, the applicable return shipping
fee is calculated and displayed to you during the return creation.
Subject to your location and the type of item, your return may be eligible for
Free Returns
.
If you choose a shipping method that entails a return shipping fee, we'll deduct
the cost of return shipping from your refund.
If you choose to ship the item on your own, you'll pay the carrier when you ship your
return.
